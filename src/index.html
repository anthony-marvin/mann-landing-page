About LASIK

96% of people are seeing 20/20 or better immediately after having LASIK at Mann Eye Institute.

This procedure is generally safe, effective and has very few side effects. It involves sophisticated laser technology in order to treat the three most common vision issues:

  • Nearsightedness (you can’t see far away)
  • Farsightedness (you can’t see up close)
  • Astigmatism (blurring at both distances)

If you’re wearing glasses and/or contacts, and you fall into one (maybe two) of these categories, then LASIK may be the right choice for you. Once upon a time, LASIK involved blades (some places still use blades so be on the lookout). Today, at Mann Eye Institute, that’s NOT the case – our LASIK is all laser, all the time.

What does that mean for you? Better outcomes, fewer side effects. Win, win.
